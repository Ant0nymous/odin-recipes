Sweetcorn Fritters

The perfect quick and easy recipe for a cold winter day

Ingredients

  • ​¾ cup Plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 440g can cream-style corn
  • 2 Tbsps oil

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and pepper to taste into a bowl.
  2. Add the beaten egg, mixing to combine. Stir in the sweetcorn.
  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of fritter mixture into the pan. Cook until golden then turn and cook the other side.
  5. Drain on absorbent paper. Serve hot.
